Who We Are

At Mera Ghar, we believe that every child deserves a life of dignity, opportunity, and love. Since 2017, we have transformed from a humble shelter into a nurturing home for over 200 bright, brave girls in Rawalpindi. Here, we don't just provide food and shelter — we build futures.

Our mission is to cultivate confident, educated, and empowered young women who shape tomorrow's society. With a focus on academic achievement, psychological care, and life skills, our girls flourish in a safe and joyful environment. Every smile, every milestone, and every dream fulfilled fuels our commitment to grow.
